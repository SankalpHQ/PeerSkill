The PeerSkill Advantage

PeerSkill is the platform that democratizes skill learning. We believe that everyone has something to teach and something to learn. By using a credits-based system, we enable fair and accessible skill exchange within student communities, without the burden of money.

It’s more than just a platform; it’s a community where students empower each other to grow and succeed.

How It Works

Create Your Profile

List the skills you can teach and the ones you want to learn. It's that simple.

Find Your Match

Browse skills available in your community and find a perfect peer to learn from.

Exchange Credits

Earn credits by teaching and spend them to learn, keeping every exchange fair and balanced.

Schedule Sessions

Use our in-app calendar to easily book and set up online or offline sessions.

Build Trust

Rate and review each other after a session to build a trustworthy and reliable community.

Get Rewarded

Earn badges and streaks for consistent teaching and learning to unlock new achievements.
